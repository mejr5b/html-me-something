My Time in the Military

In April 2013, when I was 17, I joined the Army National Guard. I signed up for a 6 year contract. I left for basic training and AIT directly after graduating highschool. For the first two
and a half months, I was in South Carolina at Fort Jackson. The remainder of the time I was in Virginia at Fort Lee. At Fort Lee, I went through MOS training for 92Y
which means, "Supply Specialist". This training taught me how to control the entire unit's supply purchasing and inventory. The total inventory for my unit was over $300,000,000.

Why I Joined

The reason I joined the Guard was because I was not sure what I wanted to major in at college. I knew I wanted to go to college but I was unsure what I wanted to do once I got there.
By joining the guard, I was able to take a break between highschool and college. The guard also offered tuition reimbursement.

Once I graduated from Fort Lee, I then came back to Missouri and was enrolled in the University of Missouri-Columbia. Also known as the beloved "Mizzou".
